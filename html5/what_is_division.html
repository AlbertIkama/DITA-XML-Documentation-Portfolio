What is division

Here you will learn what is division.

Division is a mathematical operation that breaks down larger numbers into smaller groups having the same number of items. The operation is denoted by (รท).

Example: If 40 athletes in a sports event need to be divided into groups of 5 , then how many groups will be formed?

Solution: Here we need to divide 40 by 5. The result will be 40 รท 5 = 8.

So, there will be 8 groups of 5 athletes in each group.
